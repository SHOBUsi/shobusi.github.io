// 01 — About

The person behind the profile

My path into cybersecurity wasn't academic — it was personal. A phishing attack compromised my own account and rather than just feeling victimised, I became obsessed with understanding how it happened and how to stop it.

That curiosity drove me through a B.Tech specialising in Cybersecurity & Forensics, and now an MSc at Northumbria University's London campus — where I've turned that obsession into research, published work, and hands-on offensive security practice.

I'm working toward building an independent cybersecurity consultancy offering penetration testing, network architecture review, and digital forensics — with a particular interest in supporting law enforcement and public sector organisations.

Currently open to graduate penetration testing, SOC analyst, security consultant, and security analyst roles across the UK.
